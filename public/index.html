About MagicButton

Upload Your Meals

"Write down" your list for the last time. Create a name for each meal and list the ingredient you'd need from the store. We'll store this information and you can adjust it anytime.

Select Tags for Meals

Assign tags to meals, to allow for easy customization of meal-planning. For example, tag certain meals as "Fast" and select that tag for the nights your schedule is busiest.

One Click Meal Planning

Create your meal plan and grocery list with one click. Confirm, and get the grocery list emailed to you. "Task Rabbit"-™ integration coming soon, to get your groceries delivered to you.
